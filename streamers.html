Ali Kabbani, more commonly known as Myth or TSM Myth, is an American Twitch streamer and professional Fortnite Battle Royale player. He is among the most popular streamers on Twitch with over 6.9 million followers and over 124 million views. Kabbani also has over 4.6 million subscribers on his YouTube channel.

Kabbani plays for Team SoloMid in a team that consists of himself, Daequan, Darryle "Hamlinz" Hamlin and Juan "CaMiLLs" Camilla (sub). Kabbani joined Team SoloMid in 2018 and serves as the captain of his team. Kabbani participated in the Ninja Vegas Tournament in April 2018. Additionally, Kabbani's streams have included a variety of other popular streamers including, Pokimane and summit1g. Kabbani compares Fortnite building/editing mechanics to a shooter version of chess.

Rachelle Hofstetter is an American video-game streamer known as Valkyrae. She is a full time content creator for Youtube. Valkyrae is part of an Esports team called 100 Thieves. She is mostly known for her varierty of different games and genres.

Hofstetter was born in Washington to a German father and Filipino mother. While attending community college, she worked at GameStop. She began a gaming channel on Instagram, and soon transitioned to Twitch. After gathering a following, she began posting gaming videos on YouTube. She is the first female gamer and content creator for 100 Thieves, a competitive American esports team. Hofstetter is an advocate for women in online gaming, sometimes playing games on all-female teams. On January 13, 2020, Valkyrae left Twitch for an exclusive contract on YouTube.
